AIAssist is our fastest-growing product, now generating over 16 million in revenue and commanding the highest gross margins at 71 percent. CloudCore remains the volume leader but margin compression is becoming a concern. AMER dominates overall revenue at 20 million, nearly double LATAM which remains significantly underpenetrated despite showing the highest growth rate quarter over quarter. The trend line is unmistakable — every quarter from Q1 2023 through Q2 2024 shows consistent growth, accelerating in the back half. SecureEdge is underperforming across all regions and should be reviewed for strategic repositioning or divestment. Marketing spend efficiency is highest in APAC where we get the best return per dollar. Customer churn in the LATAM segment is running at nearly double the global average which is alarming given that we are trying to grow there. The big opportunity is doubling down on AIAssist in APAC and LATAM where penetration is still low.
